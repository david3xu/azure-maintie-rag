# Equipment Maintenance Procedures

## Daily Inspection Checklist
Check all equipment for unusual noises, vibrations, or leaks.
Monitor pressure gauges and temperature readings.
Verify safety systems are operational.

## Common Issues and Solutions
1. Pump cavitation - Check suction line for blockages
2. Motor overheating - Verify cooling system operation
3. Seal leakage - Inspect mechanical seals for wear

## Safety Procedures
Always isolate equipment before maintenance.
Use proper lockout/tagout procedures.
Wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
